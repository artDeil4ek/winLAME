Ogg Vorbis Settings Page

This is the settings page for generating Ogg Vorbis audio files. Ogg Vorbis encoding and decoding is only available if the file "libvorbis.dll" is present in the winLAME folder.

Quality Management

Quality Settings: lets you choose a quality factor from 0.0 (bad quality) to 10.0 (excellent quality) for the encoding process. A value of 4.0 normally yields good results. The estimated approximate bitrate is shown to the right.

Bitrate Settings

Note: These modes are generally not recommended to use for normal encoding!

variable Bitrate: lets you specify a minimum and maximum bitrate the encoder takes as base for encoding.

average Bitrate: lets you specify a nominal bitrate. Target bitrate will always be approximately at the nominal bitrate.

constant Bitrate: lets you specify a constant bitrate that will be ensured in the whole stream.
